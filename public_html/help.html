Friend Finder

Overview

This website is designed to help people find new friends. After creating an account you are encouraged to edit your profile. After editing your profile, Friend Finder will attempt to match you with potential friends based on location, interests, and age. You will have the oportunity to message with these matches or skip them.




Create Account


The create account page has three input boxes. The first box is your full name. The second box is your username. The third box is your password.




Login


You can use the login page to loginto your account using your username and password.




Home


The home page is where you will be able to match with people. From the homepage, and most other pages, you can access your messages and your profile by clicking on the buttons in the top right. If you ever want to return to the home page just click on the friend finder title on the top left.




Edit Profile


This is where you can edit your profile. Just type relevant information into the textboxes, then select: "save profile".




Messages


This is where you can message your new friends. On the left there is a list of ongoing messages. If you click on one of these you will be able to send new messages on the left.
